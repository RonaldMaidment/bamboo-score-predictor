One entry per person per match. Winners must be present at Bamboo Bar and Grill when the result is announced to collect their prize. If two or more entrants predict the correct score, the prize will be divided equally between all winners. If no correct prediction is made, the prize jackpots and carries over to the next match. Staff and their immediate family are not eligible. Management decision is final in all matters.

How To Use
Step by step guide for each game day.
Before The Match
  • 🏈
    Set Match - go to Set Match and enter teams, date and prize
  • 📋
    Update Google Form - add the match to the dropdown at forms.google.com
  • 📷
    Print poster - go to Game Day Poster tab and click Print
  • 📱
    Display QR codes - put posters around the venue
After The Match
  • 🏆
    Enter Result - go to Enter Result and type the winning score
  • 🎉
    Check winners - the app will show any matching entries
  • 📱
    Contact winner - use their phone or email from your Google Sheet
  • 📋
    Update Sheet - mark Status column as Paid out or Jackpotted
